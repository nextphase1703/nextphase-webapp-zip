Operations

Our ISO 9001-compliant operations focus on speed, quality, and efficiency. Specialized global logistics and shipping hubs provide our customers with the necessary resources to promote small and large-scale projects all over the world.

AS9120: The Aerospace Standard (AS) 9120 standard is a quality management system (QMS) developed specifically for the aerospace industry. Compliance with AS9120 ensures that we meet the rigorous quality and safety requirements of the aerospace industry.

RoHS: The Restriction of Hazardous Substances (RoHS) Directive restricts the use of certain hazardous substances in electronic and electrical equipment sold in the European Union. RoHS compliance ensures that electronic components are free from lead, mercury, cadmium, and other harmful substances.

By implementing an ISMS based on the ISO 27001 standard, we can ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our sensitive information. Compliance with ISO 27001 is also essential for organizations that handle sensitive information or have regulatory or contractual obligations related to information security. To achieve compliance with ISO 27001, we must implement the necessary policies, procedures, and controls, undergo regular audits, and continuously review and update our practices to ensure ongoing compliance.

By implementing an EMS based on the ISO 14001:2004 standard, we can effectively manage our environmental impacts, reduce our environmental footprint, and comply with environmental regulations. Compliance with ISO 14001:2004 is also essential for organizations that seek to demonstrate their commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Market Intelligence

The market for electronics is ever-changing. Analyzing market shifts and product trends is vital. We provide market intelligence services to our clients to help them avoid unexpected component shortages and market turbulence.

Some examples of the market intelligence services we provide include:

  1. Supply Chain Analysis: We analyze supply chain data to identify potential bottlenecks and risks that could impact our clients' operations. This helps our clients make informed decisions about sourcing components and managing their supply chain.
  2. Competitor Analysis: We monitor our clients' competitors and provide insights into their strategies and market positioning. This helps our clients stay ahead of the competition and make strategic decisions to grow their business.
  3. Product Trends Analysis: We track product trends and emerging technologies to help our clients stay ahead of the curve. This enables our clients to introduce new products and services to the market before their competitors do.
  4. Component Shortage Alerts: We provide real-time alerts to our clients about component shortages that could impact their operations. This enables our clients to take proactive steps to avoid supply chain disruptions and maintain their production schedules.
  5. Regulatory Compliance Alerts: We monitor changes in regulations and compliance standards to help our clients stay compliant with the latest requirements. This reduces the risk of penalties and other legal issues that could impact our clients' operations.

Our market intelligence services are designed to help our clients stay ahead of the curve and make informed decisions about their operations.
